We're sorry to see you considering leaving Aegis Live

We truly value the time you've spent with Aegis Live, and we're sorry to see you considering leaving. Deleting your account is a permanent step, and we want to make sure it's the right choice for you.

If there's anything we can do to improve your experience or address any concerns, please reach out to our support team at support@aegislive.com—we're here to listen and help.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

  • Permanent Removal: Your account, including your profile, videos, live streams, comments, and any associated data, will be permanently deleted from our systems. This process cannot be undone.
  • Data Retention: Some anonymized data may be retained for legal or security purposes, as outlined in our Privacy Policy, but it will no longer be linked to you.
  • Impact on Connections: Your content will no longer be visible to other users, and any interactions (like follows or messages) may be affected.
  • Third-Party Access: If you've connected your account to other services, you may need to manage those separately.
